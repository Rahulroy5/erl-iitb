We welcome motivated researchers who want to work on environmental engineering challenges
with real-world impact. Below are the types of positions typically available.

> **Current openings:** Please email Prof. Chandel directly with your CV and a brief statement
> of your research interests. Positions are filled on a rolling basis.

---

## Ph.D. Positions

**Eligibility:**
- M.Tech. / M.E. in Environmental Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering,
  Biotechnology, or a closely related field
- GATE score (for Institute Fellowship) or equivalent fellowship (UGC/CSIR/DBT/INSPIRE)
- Strong fundamentals in either biological processes, chemical processes, or environmental systems

**What we look for:**
- Genuine curiosity about environmental problems — not just a degree
- Ability to work independently and handle ambiguity
- Prior thesis or project work in a relevant area is a strong plus

**How to apply:**  
Email [munish@iitb.ac.in](mailto:munish@iitb.ac.in) with subject line `PhD Application – [Your Name]`.  
Attach: CV, GATE scorecard, M.Tech. thesis abstract (if available), and a 1-page statement of
research interest.

---

## M.Tech. Project Students

IIT Bombay M.Tech. students from ESED and related departments can undertake their dissertation
project at ERL. Projects span experimental, computational, and field-based work.

**How to apply:**  
Speak to Prof. Chandel directly or email [munish@iitb.ac.in](mailto:munish@iitb.ac.in) during
the project allocation period each semester.

---

## Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoc positions are available when funded projects are active. Candidates with strong
publication records in environmental engineering, biotechnology, or LCA are encouraged to reach
out even when no position is formally advertised.

**Eligibility:**
- Ph.D. in a relevant field
- At least one first-author publication in a peer-reviewed journal

**How to apply:**  
Email [munish@iitb.ac.in](mailto:munish@iitb.ac.in) with subject `Postdoc Inquiry – [Your Name]`.  
Attach: CV with full publication list and a brief research proposal (1–2 pages).

---

## Research Internships

Occasional short-term research internships (2–6 months) are available for highly motivated
undergraduate and master's students from IIT Bombay and other institutions.

Preference is given to students with prior lab experience and a clear idea of what they want to learn.

---

_ERL is committed to an inclusive research environment. We encourage applications from all backgrounds._
