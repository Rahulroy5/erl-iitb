## For Students Applying

Please read the [Opportunities](/opportunities/) page carefully before emailing.

to receive a response.

For media inquiries, industry collaborations, or funded project proposals, email
[munish@iitb.ac.in](mailto:munish@iitb.ac.in) with the subject line indicating your purpose.
