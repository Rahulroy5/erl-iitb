Our research addresses critical environmental challenges through rigorous engineering and science.
We work across the full chain — from fundamental process understanding to technology development
and policy-relevant assessment.

---

## Waste Management & Treatment Technologies

Solid waste in Indian cities is heterogeneous, rapidly growing, and poorly characterized. Our
work develops robust characterization methods, treatment technology assessments, and
decision-support frameworks for municipal and industrial solid waste.

**Key themes:**
- Composition and characterization of municipal solid waste (MSW)
- Comparative assessment of waste-to-energy and biological treatment pathways
- Policy and regulatory frameworks for sustainable waste management
- Informal sector integration in solid waste management

---

## Biogas Production & Anaerobic Digestion

We study the anaerobic digestion process to maximize biogas yield from organic waste streams —
including food waste, agricultural residues, and sewage sludge. Our work covers both
fundamental microbiology and process engineering optimization.

**Key themes:**
- Mono and co-digestion of organic substrates
- Pre-treatment methods to enhance digestibility
- Kinetic modeling of anaerobic digestion
- Biogas upgrading and utilization pathways

---

## Sewage Sludge Valorization

Sewage sludge from wastewater treatment plants is a complex waste stream containing energy,
nutrients (N, P), and organic matter. Rather than treating it as a disposal problem, we
investigate pathways for recovering these resources.

**Key themes:**
- Sludge dewatering and stabilization
- Nutrient recovery (struvite precipitation, phosphorus extraction)
- Thermal and biological co-treatment pathways
- Heavy metal speciation and risk assessment

---

## Life Cycle Assessment & Circular Economy

We apply LCA methodology to evaluate the environmental footprint of waste management and
industrial systems, and use circular economy frameworks to identify resource recovery
opportunities at city and sector scale.

**Key themes:**
- Comparative LCA of waste treatment scenarios
- Carbon footprint and greenhouse gas accounting
- Circular economy metrics and indicator development
- Industrial symbiosis and urban metabolism

---

## Environmental Impact Assessment

Assessment of pollution from industrial and urban sources, including air, water, and soil
contamination. We develop field measurement, modeling, and risk assessment methodologies.

**Key themes:**
- Air quality monitoring and source apportionment
- Groundwater contamination from landfills and industrial sites
- Environmental health risk assessment
- Regulatory compliance and environmental auditing
